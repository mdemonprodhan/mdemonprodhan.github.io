Short Biography

Md. Emon Prodhan

  • I am an early-career statistician and researcher from Sylhet, Bangladesh, currently pursuing my Master of Science in Statistics at Shahjalal University of Science and Technology (SUST). My research sits at the intersection of public health, social science, and data science. I am particularly interested in evidence-informed research, child health, women's empowerment, and the application of machine learning methods to health and environmental data.
  • I completed my B.Sc. (Honours) in Statistics from SUST with a CGPA of 3.84 (Distinction), and my undergraduate project examined the determinants of early childhood mortality in Bangladesh.
  • I am well-versed in literature reviews, statistical analysis, academic writing, and the dissemination of research in peer-reviewed publications and conference presentations. I am particularly interested in studies and policies related to evidence and evidence-informed research, civil society, and collaborative international research.
